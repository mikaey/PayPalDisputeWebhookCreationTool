Dispute Webhook Creation Tool

This tool will subscribe you to the CUSTOMER.DISPUTE.CREATED, CUSTOMER.DISPUTE.UPDATED, and CUSTOMER.DISPUTE.RESOLVED webhook events in your live account. Additionally, it will create an account lookup, so that you will receive webhooks for transactions that were not initiated through the PayPal REST APIs.
